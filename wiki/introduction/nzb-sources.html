--- title: NZB Sources ---

SABnzbd does not browser Usenet headers but instead only accepts NZB files to process 1. An NZB file contains a full specification of all Usenet articles that are needed for a download. You can get these files from sites listed below.

SABnzbd does not endorse or require the use of any specific indexer.
They all work with SABnzbd perfectly fine so long as they generate NZBs, none of these sites require specific "SABnzbd integration".
Before paying for an indexer please keep in mind that there are no guarantees and that any site could disappear at any time without notice.

Raw search engines

These are classic search engines that index and give you results by key words. No human influence, which means little to no logic on filtering out bad posts (spam/virus/password protected/etc) or un-obfuscating encrypted posts. Just an algorithm to search.

Be careful when obtaining NZBs through this method - user discretion is advised.
